# Bactrim (Trimethoprim / Sulfamethoxazole)

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Sulfonamide antibiotic / Dihydrofolate reductase inhibitor |
| Formulation | Suspension (40/200 mg per 5 mL) or Tablets (80/400 mg) |
| Concentration | Suspension: Trimethoprim 8 mg / Sulfamethoxazole 40 mg per mL |
| Storage | Room temp |

---

## Oral Graded Challenge (OGC)

!!! info "Suspension Preparation"
    Use **Bactrim Suspension (40 mg / 200 mg per 5 mL)** which provides **8 mg / 40 mg per 1 mL**.

### Protocol
| Step | Dose (TMP/SMX) | Volume (Suspension) | Cumulative Dose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 16 mg / 80 mg | 2.0 mL | 16 mg / 80 mg |
| 2 | 64 mg / 320 mg | 8.0 mL | 80 mg / 400 mg |
| 3 | 80 mg / 400 mg | 10.0 mL | 160 mg / 800 mg |

**Interval:** 30–60 minutes between steps as per clinical orders.

---

## Oral Ungraded Challenge
For patients with a low-risk history or as a final validation step.

### Protocol
| Step | Dose (TMP/SMX) | Formulation | Cumulative Dose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 80 mg / 400 mg | 1 × Tablet | 80 mg / 400 mg |

---

## Intravenous Skin Testing
*Note: IV Bactrim skin testing is less common and protocols vary based on clinical need. Consult senior staff if IV testing is required.*
