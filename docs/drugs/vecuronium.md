# Vecuronium

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Neuromuscular blocking agent (NMBA) |
| Stock vial | 10 mg powder for injection |
| Reconstituted conc. | 4 mg/mL (add 2.5 mL WFI) |
| Storage | Room temp before; 2–8°C after |

---

## Preparation

!!! info "Before you start"
    Ensure the vial contains **10 mg** of Vecuronium. Check expiry and integrity. Note that this requires 2.5 mL of diluent.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Vecuronium 10 mg vial
- Water for injection (WFI) — at least 2.5 mL
- Normal saline (NS) — for IDT dilutions
- 5 mL syringe (for reconstitution)
- 1 mL syringes × 4 (for test doses)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Reconstitution
1. Reconstitute the **10 mg vial** with **2.5 mL WFI**.
2. Dissolve completely to obtain **Vecuronium 4 mg/mL** (Neat).

### Step 2 — Prepare the SPT solution (4 mg/mL — neat)
1. Draw up **0.5–1 mL** of the neat (4 mg/mL) solution into a 1 mL syringe.
2. Label: **"Vecuronium SPT 4 mg/mL"**.

### Step 3 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 0.04 mg/mL (1:100):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of neat (4 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 0.4 mg/mL — 1:10).
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of this 0.4 mg/mL into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.04 mg/mL — 1:100**).
    - Label: **"Vecuronium IDT 0.04 mg/mL"**.

2. **IDT 0.004 mg/mL (1:1,000):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 0.04 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.004 mg/mL — 1:1,000**).
    - Label: **"Vecuronium IDT 0.004 mg/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (4 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:1,000 | 0.004 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.04 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 2 | 1:100 | 0.04 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.4 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.
