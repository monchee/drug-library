# Rosuvastatin (Crestor)

## Overview
| Field | Detail |

| Reconstituted conc. | N/A (Oral) |

---

## Preparation (Desensitisation)

!!! info "Protocol Context"
    Rosuvastatin desensitisation is typically performed for patients with a history of non-severe hypersensitivity who require statin therapy. This protocol uses standard 10 mg tablets.

### Equipment needed
- 3 × Rosuvastatin 10 mg tablets
- Water for swallowing
- Observation chart

---

## Desensitisation Protocol

### Dosing Schedule
| Step | Dose | Formulation | Cumulative Dose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 10 mg | 1 × Tablet | 10 mg |
| 2 | 10 mg | 1 × Tablet | 20 mg |
| 3 | 10 mg | 1 × Tablet | 30 mg |

**Interval:** 20 minutes between doses.
**Total Dose:** 30 mg.

!!! tip "Post-Protocol"
    If the desensitisation is successful, the patient should continue their prescribed daily dose (e.g., 10 mg or 20 mg) starting the following day without interruption.

---

## Notes
- Monitor for signs of hypersensitivity (rash, itch, respiratory distress) between steps.
- This protocol is specific for immediate-type reactions. For delayed muscle symptoms (SAMS), a different clinical approach is required.
