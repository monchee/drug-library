# Thiopental (Pentothal)

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Ultra-short-acting barbiturate / Hypnotic agent |
| Stock vial | 500 mg powder for injection |
| Reconstituted conc. | 25 mg/mL (add 20 mL WFI) |
| Storage | Room temp before reconstitution; 2–8°C after |

---

## Preparation

!!! warning "Reconstitution"
    Thiopental follows a specific 25 mg/mL reconstitution for testing. Ensure the 20 mL of Water for Injections (WFI) is added slowly and the solution is clear before use.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Thiopental 500 mg powder vial
- Water for Injections (WFI) or Normal saline (NS) — for reconstitution
- Normal saline (NS) — for IDT dilutions
- 10 mL and 20 mL syringes
- 1 mL syringes × 4 (for test doses/dilutions)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Reconstitute the stock (25 mg/mL)
1. Add **20 mL** of WFI to the 500 mg powder vial.
2. Shake gently until completely dissolved. (This is **25 mg/mL**).
3. Draw up **0.5–1 mL** into a 1 mL syringe.
4. Label: **"Thiopental SPT 25 mg/mL"**.

### Step 2 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 2.5 mg/mL (1:10):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of stock (25 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **2.5 mg/mL — 1:10**).
    - Label: **"Thiopental IDT 2.5 mg/mL"**.

2. **IDT 0.25 mg/mL (1:100):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 2.5 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.25 mg/mL — 1:100**).
    - Label: **"Thiopental IDT 0.25 mg/mL"**.

3. **IDT 0.025 mg/mL (1:1,000):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 0.25 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.025 mg/mL — 1:1,000**).
    - Label: **"Thiopental IDT 0.025 mg/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (25 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:1,000 | 0.025 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.25 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 2 | 1:100 | 0.25 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 2.5 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 3 | 1:10 | 2.5 mg/mL | 0.1 mL neat + 0.9 mL NS |

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.
