# Dalteparin (Fragmin)

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Low molecular weight heparin (LMWH) |
| Stock vial | 10,000 U/mL pre-filled syringe or vial |
| Reconstituted conc. | N/A (Ready to use) |
| Storage | Room temp |

---

## Preparation

!!! info "Before you start"
    Ensure the vial or syringe contains **10,000 U/mL**. Check expiry and integrity. Note that this is a common cause of delayed-type hypersensitivity (DTH) reactions.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Dalteparin 10,000 U/mL syringe/vial
- Normal saline (NS) — for IDT dilutions
- 1 mL syringes × 5 (for test doses/dilutions)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Prepare the SPT solution (10,000 U/mL — neat)
1. Draw up **0.5–1 mL** of the stock (10,000 U/mL) into a 1 mL syringe.
2. Label: **"Dalteparin SPT 10,000 U/mL"**.

### Step 2 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 1,000 U/mL (1:10):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of stock (10,000 U/mL) into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **1,000 U/mL — 1:10**).
    - Label: **"Dalteparin IDT 1,000 U/mL"**.

2. **IDT 100 U/mL (1:100):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 1,000 U/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **100 U/mL — 1:100**).
    - Label: **"Dalteparin IDT 100 U/mL"**.

3. **IDT 10 U/mL (1:1,000):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 100 U/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **10 U/mL — 1:1,000**).
    - Label: **"Dalteparin IDT 10 U/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (10,000 U/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:1,000 | 10 U/mL | 0.1 mL of 100 U/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 2 | 1:100 | 100 U/mL | 0.1 mL of 1,000 U/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 3 | 1:10 | 1,000 U/mL | 0.1 mL neat + 0.9 mL NS |

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.
