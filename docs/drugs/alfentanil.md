## Preparation

!!! info "Before you start"
    Ensure the vial contains **1 mg/2 mL (0.5 mg/mL)**. Check expiry and integrity. Note that Alfentanil is a Schedule 8 (S8) medication and requires appropriate handling and witnessing.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Alfentanil 1 mg/2 mL vial
- Normal saline (NS) — for IDT dilutions
- 1 mL syringes × 3 (for test doses)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Prepare the SPT solution (0.5 mg/mL — neat)
1. Draw up **0.5–1 mL** of the stock (0.5 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe.
2. Label: **"Alfentanil SPT 0.5 mg/mL"**.

### Step 2 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 0.05 mg/mL (1:10):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of stock (0.5 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.05 mg/mL — 1:10**).
    - Label: **"Alfentanil IDT 0.05 mg/mL"**.

2. **IDT 0.005 mg/mL (1:100):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 0.05 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.005 mg/mL — 1:100**).
    - Label: **"Alfentanil IDT 0.005 mg/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (0.5 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:100 | 0.005 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.05 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 2 | 1:10 | 0.05 mg/mL | 0.1 mL neat + 0.9 mL NS |

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.
