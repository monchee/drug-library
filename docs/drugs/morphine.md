---

## Preparation

!!! info "Before you start"
    Ensure the vial contains **10 mg/mL**. Check expiry and integrity. Note that Morphine is a Schedule 8 (S8) medication and requires appropriate handling and witnessing. **SPT is NOT performed neat for Morphine** as it is a potent histamine-releaser.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Morphine 10 mg/mL vial
- Normal saline (NS) — for dilutions
- 1 mL syringes × 5 (for test doses/dilutions)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Prepare the SPT solution (1 mg/mL — 1:10)
1. Draw up **0.1 mL** of the stock (10 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe.
2. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **1 mg/mL — 1:10**).
3. Label: **"Morphine SPT 1 mg/mL (1:10)"**.

### Step 2 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 0.0001 mg/mL (1:100,000):**
    - **A:** Draw **0.1 mL** of stock (10 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 1 mg/mL — 1:10).
    - **B:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **A** (1 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 0.1 mg/mL — 1:100).
    - **C:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **B** (0.1 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 0.01 mg/mL — 1:1,000).
    - **D:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **C** (0.01 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 0.001 mg/mL — 1:10,000).
    - **E:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **D** (0.001 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.0001 mg/mL — 1:100,000**).
    - Label: **"Morphine IDT 0.0001 mg/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | 1:10 (1 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:100,000 | 0.0001 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.001 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |

!!! warning "Potent Histamine Releaser"
    Morphine is a potent direct histamine releaser. Results must be interpreted with caution. IDT should be performed at very high dilutions (**1:100,000**) as per the institutional protocol.

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.
