# Cefuroxime

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | 2nd generation cephalosporin |
| Route | Oral (challenge); IV (SPT/IDT) |
| Forms | 250 mg, 500 mg tablets; 125 mg/5 mL suspension |
| Stock (IV) | 750 mg powder for injection or 125 mg/5 mL suspension |
| Reconstituted conc. | 25 mg/mL (750 mg + 30 mL NS) or use 125 mg/5 mL (25 mg/mL) suspension |
| Diluent | Normal saline (NS) |
| Storage | Room temp before; refrigerate suspension after mixing |

---

## Preparation for Skin Testing

!!! info "Before you start"
    For SPT/IDT, use either the 750 mg IV vial reconstituted to 25 mg/mL, or the 125 mg/5 mL (25 mg/mL) oral suspension. Label every syringe immediately after drawing up.

### Equipment needed
- Cefuroxime 750 mg powder vial **or** 125 mg/5 mL suspension (25 mg/mL)
- Normal saline (NS) — for reconstitution and dilutions
- 30 mL syringe (if using 750 mg vial)
- 1 mL syringes × 4 (one for SPT, three for IDT dilutions)
- 25 G needles
- Labels or marker pen

### Step 1 — Prepare the stock solution (25 mg/mL)
**Option A — Using 750 mg vial:**
1. Draw up **30 mL** of NS into a 30 mL syringe.
2. Inject into the Cefuroxime 750 mg vial using aseptic technique.
3. Gently swirl until fully dissolved. The resulting stock is **25 mg/mL**.

**Option B — Using 125 mg/5 mL suspension:**
1. The suspension is already **25 mg/mL**. Use directly.

### Step 2 — Prepare the SPT solution (25 mg/mL — neat)
1. Draw up **0.5–1 mL** of the 25 mg/mL stock into a 1 mL syringe.
2. Label: **"Cefuroxime SPT 25 mg/mL"**.

### Step 3 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 2.5 mg/mL (1:10):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of stock (25 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **2.5 mg/mL — 1:10**).
    - Label: **"Cefuroxime IDT 2.5 mg/mL"**.

2. **IDT 0.25 mg/mL (1:100):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 2.5 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.25 mg/mL — 1:100**).
    - Label: **"Cefuroxime IDT 0.25 mg/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (25 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:100 | 0.25 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 2.5 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 2 | 1:10 | 2.5 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 25 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.

## Preparation for Oral Challenge

!!! info "Before you start"
    Ensure the patient has fasted if required. Use a calibrated oral syringe for all volumes. Check the concentration of the suspension (typically 125 mg/5 mL).

### Reagents
- Cefuroxime Suspension **125 mg/5 mL** (25 mg/mL)
- Syringes: 1 mL, 5 mL oral syringes

### Step-by-step Challenge Protocol

#### Using 125 mg/5 mL (25 mg/mL) Suspension:
| Step | Dose | Volume | Cumulative Dose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 25 mg | 1.0 mL | 25 mg |
| 2 | 100 mg | 4.0 mL | 125 mg |

---

## Challenge Protocol
**Intervals:** Observe for **30–60 minutes** between each step.
**Final Observation:** Monitor for **1–2 hours** after the final dose.
