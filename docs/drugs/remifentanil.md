# Remifentanil

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Ultra-short-acting synthetic opioid analgesic |
| Stock vial | 1 mg powder for injection |
| Reconstituted conc. | 0.05 mg/mL (add 20 mL WFI) |
| Storage | Room temp before; 2–8°C after |

---

## Preparation

!!! info "Before you start"
    Ensure the vial contains **1 mg** of Remifentanil. Check expiry and integrity. Note that Remifentanil is a Schedule 8 (S8) medication and requires appropriate handling and witnessing. This requires **20 mL** of diluent for the standard 0.05 mg/mL concentration.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Remifentanil 1 mg vial
- Water for injection (WFI) — at least 20 mL
- Normal saline (NS) — for IDT dilutions
- 20 mL syringe (for reconstitution)
- 1 mL syringes × 3 (for test doses)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Reconstitution
1. Reconstitute the **1 mg vial** with **20 mL WFI**.
2. Dissolve completely to obtain **Remifentanil 0.05 mg/mL** (Neat).

### Step 2 — Prepare the SPT solution (0.05 mg/mL — neat)
1. Draw up **0.5–1 mL** of the neat (0.05 mg/mL) solution into a 1 mL syringe.
2. Label: **"Remifentanil SPT 0.05 mg/mL"**.

### Step 3 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 0.005 mg/mL (1:10):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of neat (0.05 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.005 mg/mL — 1:10**).
    - Label: **"Remifentanil IDT 0.005 mg/mL"**.

2. **IDT 0.0005 mg/mL (1:100):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 0.005 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.0005 mg/mL — 1:100**).
    - Label: **"Remifentanil IDT 0.0005 mg/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (0.05 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:100 | 0.0005 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.005 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 2 | 1:10 | 0.005 mg/mL | 0.1 mL neat + 0.9 mL NS |

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.
