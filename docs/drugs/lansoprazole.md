# Lansoprazole (Zoton)

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Proton Pump Inhibitor (PPI) |
| Formulation | 30 mg capsules / orodispersible tablets |
| Stock concentration | 30 mg/mL (reconstituted for testing) |
| Storage | Room temp |

---

## Preparation

!!! info "Testing Method"
    PPI testing is typically performed via **Oral Graded Challenge (OGC)**. Skin testing (SPT) with reconstituted oral formulations (30 mg/mL Neat) may be performed but has limited validated sensitivity.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Lansoprazole 30 mg tablet/capsule
- Water for swallowing
- Observation chart

---

## Oral Graded Challenge (OGC)
| Step | Dose | Formulation | Cumulative Dose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 3 mg | 1/10 Tablet (or liquid equiv) | 3 mg |
| 2 | 30 mg | 1 Tablet | 33 mg |

**Interval:** 20 minutes between steps.

---

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (30 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Preparation:** Dissolve 30 mg in 1 mL NS. Use the supernatant for SPT.
**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.
