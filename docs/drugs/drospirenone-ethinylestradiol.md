# Drospirenone / Ethinylestradiol (Yaz)

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Hormonal contraceptive |
| Route | Oral |
| Stock vial | Drospirenone 3 mg / Ethinylestradiol 20 µg tablet (Yaz) |
| Reconstituted conc. | Crush tablet, dissolve in NS for testing |
| Diluent | Nil |
| Storage | Room temp; Protect from light and moisture |

!!! note
    SPT only — no IDT protocol available for this drug. Tablets must be crushed and dissolved in normal saline to prepare the SPT test solution.

---

## Preparation

!!! info "Before you start"
    Check the expiry date and blister pack integrity before opening. Label every syringe immediately after drawing up — never leave unlabelled syringes on the tray.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Yaz tablet (Drospirenone 3 mg / Ethinylestradiol 20 µg)
- Mortar and pestle (for crushing tablet)
- Normal saline (NS) 0.9% — for dissolving crushed tablet
- 1 mL syringe × 1
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Prepare the SPT solution (neat — crushed tablet solution)
1. Place one Yaz tablet into a clean mortar.
2. Crush the tablet to a fine powder using the pestle.
3. Add a small volume of NS (approximately 1–2 drops) to the powder and mix to form a paste/solution.
4. Draw up the solution into a 1 mL syringe.
5. Label: **"Yaz SPT (crushed tablet solution)"**.

---

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (crushed tablet solution) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.
