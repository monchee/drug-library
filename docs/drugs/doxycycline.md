# Doxycycline

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Tetracycline antibiotic |
| Stock formulation | 100 mg powder for injection or 100 mg tablets |
| Reconstituted conc. | 10 mg/mL (add 10 mL WFI) |
| Storage | Room temp before reconstitution; Protect from light |

---

## Preparation (IV Testing)

!!! info "Reconstitution"
    Wait for the powder to dissolve completely. The solution should be clear and yellow. Prepare IDT dilutions immediately after reconstitution.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Doxycycline 100 mg powder vial
- Water for Injections (WFI) or Normal saline (NS) — for reconstitution
- Normal saline (NS) — for IDT dilutions
- 10 mL syringe
- 1 mL syringes × 4 (for test doses/dilutions)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Reconstitute the stock (10 mg/mL)
1. Add **10 mL** of WFI or NS to the 100 mg powder vial.
2. Shake gently until completely dissolved. (This is **10 mg/mL**).
3. Draw up **0.5–1 mL** into a 1 mL syringe.
4. Label: **"Doxycycline SPT 10 mg/mL"**.

### Step 2 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 1 mg/mL (1:10):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of stock (10 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **1 mg/mL — 1:10**).
    - Label: **"Doxycycline IDT 1 mg/mL"**.

2. **IDT 0.1 mg/mL (1:100):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 1 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.1 mg/mL — 1:100**).
    - Label: **"Doxycycline IDT 0.1 mg/mL"**.

3. **IDT 0.01 mg/mL (1:1,000):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 0.1 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.01 mg/mL — 1:1,000**).
    - Label: **"Doxycycline IDT 0.01 mg/mL"**.

4. **IDT 0.001 mg/mL (1:10,000):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 0.01 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.001 mg/mL — 1:10,000**).
    - Label: **"Doxycycline IDT 0.001 mg/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (10 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:10,000 | 0.001 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.01 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 2 | 1:1,000 | 0.01 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.1 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 3 | 1:100 | 0.1 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 1 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 4 | 1:10 | 1 mg/mL | 0.1 mL neat + 0.9 mL NS |

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.

## Oral Graded Challenge (OGC)
*Using APO-Doxycycline 100 mg tablets.*

### Protocol
| Step | Dose | Formulation | Cumulative Dose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 25 mg | 1/4 Tablet | 25 mg |
| 2 | 50 mg | 1/2 Tablet | 75 mg |
| 3 | 100 mg | 1 Tablet | 175 mg |

**Interval:** 30–60 minutes between steps.
