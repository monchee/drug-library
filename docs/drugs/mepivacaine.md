# Mepivacaine

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Amide local anaesthetic |
| Stock vial | 66 mg/2.2 mL (30 mg/mL) or 3% solution |
| Reconstituted conc. | 10 mg/mL (add 4.4 mL WFI to 2.2 mL stock) |
| Storage | Room temp; Do not freeze |

---

## Preparation

!!! info "Before you start"
    Ensure the vial contains **Mepivacaine 3% (30 mg/mL)**. This requiring dilution for skin testing.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Mepivacaine 30 mg/mL vial
- Water for injection (WFI) — at least 5 mL (for reconstitution)
- Normal saline (NS) — for IDT dilutions
- 10 mL syringe (for initial dilution)
- 1 mL syringes × 5 (for test doses/dilutions)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Initial Dilution (to 10 mg/mL Neat)
1. Draw up **2.2 mL** of stock (30 mg/mL = 66 mg).
2. Add **4.4 mL WFI**. Mix.
3. Total volume is 6.6 mL (66 mg / 6.6 mL = **10 mg/mL** Neat).

### Step 2 — Prepare the SPT solution (10 mg/mL)
1. Draw up **0.5–1 mL** of the 10 mg/mL solution into a 1 mL syringe.
2. Label: **"Mepivacaine SPT 10 mg/mL"**.

### Step 3 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 0.01 mg/mL (1:1,000):**
    - **A:** Draw **0.1 mL** of the 10 mg/mL Neat into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 1 mg/mL — 1:10).
    - **B:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **A** (1 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 0.1 mg/mL — 1:100).
    - **C:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **B** (0.1 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.01 mg/mL — 1:1,000**).
    - Label: **"Mepivacaine IDT 0.01 mg/mL"**.

2. **IDT 0.1 mg/mL (1:100):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of syringe **B** (0.1 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe.
    - Label: **"Mepivacaine IDT 0.1 mg/mL"**.

3. **IDT 1 mg/mL (1:10):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of syringe **A** (1 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe.
    - Label: **"Mepivacaine IDT 1 mg/mL"**.

4. **IDT 10 mg/mL (Neat):**
    - Draw up a fresh **0.5 mL** of 10 mg/mL Neat.
    - Label: **"Mepivacaine IDT 10 mg/mL — Neat"**.

---

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (10 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:1,000 | 0.01 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.1 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 2 | 1:100 | 0.1 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 1 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 3 | 1:10 | 1 mg/mL | 0.1 mL neat + 0.9 mL NS |
| 4 | Neat | 10 mg/mL | 10 mg/mL solution prepared above |

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.
