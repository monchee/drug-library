# Suxamethonium (Succinylcholine)

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Depolarising neuromuscular blocking agent |
| Stock vial | 100 mg/2 mL (50 mg/mL) |
| Reconstituted conc. | N/A (Ready to use) |
| Storage | Refrigerate (2–8°C) |

---

## Preparation

!!! info "Before you start"
    Ensure the vial contains **100 mg/2 mL (50 mg/mL)**. Check expiry and integrity. Note that SPT is **NOT** performed neat for Suxamethonium.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Suxamethonium 100 mg/2 mL vial
- Normal saline (NS) — for dilutions
- 1 mL syringes × 5 (for test doses/dilutions)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Prepare the SPT solution (10 mg/mL — 1:5)
1. Draw up **0.2 mL** of the stock (50 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe.
2. Add **0.8 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **10 mg/mL — 1:5**).
3. Label: **"Suxamethonium SPT 10 mg/mL"**.

### Step 2 — Prepare IDT dilutions
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 0.1 mg/mL (1:500):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the SPT solution (10 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 1 mg/mL — 1:50).
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of this 1 mg/mL into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.1 mg/mL — 1:500**).
    - Label: **"Suxamethonium IDT 0.1 mg/mL"**.

2. **IDT 0.01 mg/mL (1:5,000):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 0.1 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.01 mg/mL — 1:5,000**).
    - Label: **"Suxamethonium IDT 0.01 mg/mL"**.

3. **IDT 0.001 mg/mL (1:50,000):**
    - Draw **0.1 mL** of the 0.01 mg/mL syringe into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.001 mg/mL — 1:50,000**).
    - Label: **"Suxamethonium IDT 0.001 mg/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | 1:5 (10 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:50,000 | 0.001 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.01 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 2 | 1:5,000 | 0.01 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.1 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |
| 3 | 1:500 | 0.1 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 1 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.
