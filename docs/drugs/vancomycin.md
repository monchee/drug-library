# Vancomycin

## Overview
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Drug class | Glycopeptide antibiotic |
| Stock vial | 500 mg or 1 g powder for injection |
| Reconstituted conc. | 100 mg/mL (add 5 mL NS to 500 mg or 10 mL NS to 1 g) |
| Storage | Room temp before; 2–8°C after |

---

## Preparation

!!! info "Before you start"
    Ensure the vial contains **500 mg** or **1 g** of Vancomycin. Check expiry and integrity. Note that Vancomycin is a potent direct histamine-releaser and must be tested at extremely high dilutions.

### Equipment needed
- 1 × Vancomycin 500 mg or 1 g vial
- Normal saline (NS) — for all dilutions
- 10 mL syringe (for initial reconstitution)
- 1 mL syringes × 10 (for multiple serial dilutions)
- 25 G needles

### Step 1 — Initial Reconstitution (100 mg/mL)
1. Reconstitute the **500 mg vial** with **5 mL NS** (or **1 g vial** with **10 mL NS**).
2. Dissolve completely to obtain **100 mg/mL**.
3. *Note: This solution is NOT for testing.*

### Step 2 — Prepare the SPT solution (100 mg/mL)
1. Draw up **0.5–1 mL** of the neat (100 mg/mL) solution into a 1 mL syringe.
2. Label: **"Vancomycin SPT 100 mg/mL"**.

### Step 3 — Prepare IDT dilutions (1:1,000,000)
Prepare in this order, using a fresh syringe for each step.

1. **IDT 0.0001 mg/mL (1:1,000,000):**
    - **A:** Draw **0.1 mL** of stock (100 mg/mL) into a 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 10 mg/mL — 1:10).
    - **B:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **A** (10 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 1 mg/mL — 1:100).
    - **C:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **B** (1 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 0.1 mg/mL — 1:1,000).
    - **D:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **C** (0.1 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 0.01 mg/mL — 1:10,000).
    - **E:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **D** (0.01 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is 0.001 mg/mL — 1:100,000).
    - **F:** Draw **0.1 mL** of **E** (0.001 mg/mL) into a new 1 mL syringe. Add **0.9 mL NS**. Mix. (This is **0.0001 mg/mL — 1:1,000,000**).
    - Label: **"Vancomycin IDT 0.0001 mg/mL"**.

## Skin prick test (SPT)
| Reagent | Concentration |
|---|---|
| Test solution | Neat (100 mg/mL) |
| Positive control | Histamine 10 mg/mL |
| Negative control | Normal saline |

**Interpretation:** Wheal ≥3 mm vs negative control = positive.

---

## Intradermal test (IDT)

### Dilution series
| Step | Dilution | Concentration | Preparation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1:1,000,000 | 0.0001 mg/mL | 0.1 mL of 0.001 mg/mL + 0.9 mL NS |

!!! warning "Histamine Release"
    Vancomycin is a potent direct histamine releaser. Results must be interpreted with extreme caution. IDT should be performed at **1:1,000,000** as per the institutional protocol.

**Inject:** 0.05 mL intradermal. Read at 15–20 minutes.
